MSF Product Training & Troubleshooting Manual

MSF-001 ~ MSF-035 | Internal Training Website

Noise Issues

MSF-001 Inside Noise

Reason: Evap fan motor bad bearings

Solution: Replace evaporator fan motor

MSF-002 Outside Noise

Reason: Condenser fan blade/motor issue

Solution: Remove obstruction or replace fan motor

Leaking Water

MSF-003 Water inside cabinet

Reason: Drain hose clogged

Solution: Clean drain hose

MSF-004 Water on one side

Reason: Unit not level

Solution: Level the unit

MSF-005 Water leaking onto floor

Reason: Drain pan overflow / insulation issue

Solution: Install heater kit, seal insulation

Cooling Issues

MSF-022 Cabinet cold, pan warm

Reason: Missing pan or airflow issue

Solution: Install pan and adjust airflow

MSF-030 Compressor short cycling

Reason: Condenser fan not running

Solution: Replace condenser fan motor

Error Codes

MSF-034 P1 / E0

Reason: Probe failure

Solution: Replace probe

MSF-035 E3 / P3-4

Reason: Bad controller

Solution: Replace control board
